Project Description:

As a project of NYU Tisch's Useles Machine program, the work was exhibited at theBlanc gallery in New York, and it was also my first project to be exhibited at a public gallery. This project comes from the idea of "useful" and "useless". This mini claw machine will prevent you from getting the doll in. No one can get anything out of this machine! As a result, it drives people crazy who are already good at it; It's boring for people who aren't good at it.
