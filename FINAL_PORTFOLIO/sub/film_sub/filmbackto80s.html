Details of Work:

This is a live audiovisual signal processing project for experimental media art performance. I process it using my own trash video clip, manipulating in Resolume VJ Software/Signal Culture Modular Apps/Adobe Premiere and other Adobe Suite. I also remix the orginal music--Cosmic Surfin (1979 Live) by Yellow Magic Orchestra, in Reaper App. Then, I attach the video and audio together. The visual and audio have relative and connected dynamic 80's vibe.
